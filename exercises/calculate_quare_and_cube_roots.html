What is the square root of R*R.

In other words calculate \sqrt{R*R}

R

The square root x of R*R is number such that x^2=R*R.

Since R^2 = R\cdotR = R*R, the square root is R.

What is the cube root of R*R*R.

In other words calculate \sqrt[3]{R*R*R}

R

The cube root x of R*R*R is number such that x^3=R*R*R.

Since R^3 = R\cdotR\cdotR = R*R*R, the cube root is R.
